Donations update 20/03/22

We have had a few queries on donations so wanted to quickly clarify where we stand on accepting items.

We are still researching the need in Corby and will likely be starting with a small trial where we will appeal for a specific wish list of items. If this is successful, we will hopefully have the insight needed to start establishing the charity, recruiting trustees and then having the capacity to accept more items from the community.

Thank you to all that has reached out so far though, it really gives us confidence that Corby can make this work!

We will soon be looking for trustees

In order for Bear Necessities to move forward as a registered charity, we will need to recruit at least three trustees; the initial positions being a chair, a secretary and a treasurer.

Our trustees will be responsible for steering the charity towards our aims and objectives, and should be passionate about our purpose to close the gap in material deprivation within Corby. This should be evidenced either by their previous paid or voluntary work in relevant sectors.

Please email us at hello@corbybabybank.org.uk for more information.
